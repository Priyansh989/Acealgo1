About Us

Algorithm Visualizer is an interactive way and platform that visualize the algorithms in two domain i.e. Path Finding and Sort Visual algorithm. The project focuses on visualizng the algorithm and try to make easier to understand and learn the algorithm. Our goal is to design an algorithm visualizer that will be useful for the students to understand and get a better clarity of the various algorithms being used for sorting.
